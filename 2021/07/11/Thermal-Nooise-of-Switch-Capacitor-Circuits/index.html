Thermal Noise of Switch Capacitor Circuits

elike 发表于 2021-07-11

One of the main limitations of the performance of switched-capacitor (SC) circuits is noise. The sources of noise include the intrinsic noise generated in the MOS transistors, as well as the extrinsic (interference) noise originating, typically from the no-chip digital circuitry, and coupled into the sensitive analog stages via the substrate and supply or ground lines.
